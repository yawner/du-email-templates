Welcome to the new and improved @Korbel. More pics, less clutter, better info.
Great people. Great things. Ours is a legacy of innovation, prestige and outside-the-box thinking. 
 
The Korbel School of International Studies celebrates a rich history of faculty and students who have taken their turn at influencing the world in innovative ways. From Erica Chenoweth's groundbreaking book on how civil resistance works to the grad student working on groundbreaking research, Korbel has a longstanding legacy of being at the front of world change. 
 
This legacy is living and accessible. Students who come to Korbel are able to access it everyday by walking the halls and taking classes from people who have advanced their fields and promoted the common good with their work.
 
But the work can't stop there. As the world becomes more sophisticated, so do its problems. Korbel is not a museum but a marketplace of ideas. We welcome and seek to prepare a new wave of change-agents to give their best attempts at creating a new world with new solutions. 
 
It's your turn.
